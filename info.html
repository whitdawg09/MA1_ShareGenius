Summary of the Show

Grey's Anatomy takes place in Seattle!

  • Season 1: Introduces surgical interns at Seattle Grace Hospital, focusing on Meredith Grey. The season sets the foundation for Meredith's relationship with Derek Shepherd and the dynamics among the interns.
  • Season 2: The love triangle between Meredith, Derek, and Addison intensifies, while Izzie falls for patient Denny Duquette, whose tragic death profoundly impacts her.
  • Season 3: Cristina and Burke's relationship takes center stage, leading to a dramatic failed wedding, and Izzie grapples with her feelings for George, complicating their friendship.
  • Season 4: The new batch of interns, including Lexie Grey, adds complexity to the group, while Meredith and Derek’s on-again, off-again relationship continues.
  • Season 5: Izzie is diagnosed with cancer, and Derek finally proposes to Meredith, leading to their unconventional "post-it note" wedding.
  • Season 6: The hospital merges with Mercy West, creating tension among staff, and the season ends with a harrowing hospital shooting that changes everyone’s lives.
  • Season 7: The characters deal with trauma from the shooting, Meredith and Derek try for a baby, and Cristina begins to question her career after her experiences in the OR.
  • Season 8: A plane crash devastates the doctors, killing Lexie Grey and later Mark Sloan, leaving the survivors grappling with guilt and grief.
  • Season 9: The aftermath of the plane crash leads to legal battles and emotional healing, as the doctors buy the hospital to prevent its closure.
  • Season 10: Cristina leaves Seattle for a new opportunity in Zurich, while the hospital faces a major storm that brings medical and emotional challenges.
  • Season 11: Meredith’s half-sister Maggie joins the hospital, while Derek’s death in a car accident marks one of the show’s most emotional moments.
  • Season 12: Meredith learns to cope with life after Derek, finding new strength and purpose as she returns to her work at the hospital.
  • Season 13: Alex faces legal trouble after assaulting DeLuca, while Meredith navigates new relationships and challenges as a single mother.
  • Season 14: Jo faces her abusive ex-husband, leading to personal growth, and April leaves the hospital after experiencing a renewed faith.
  • Season 15: The "season of love" introduces new romances, particularly Meredith’s with Andrew DeLuca, and deals with the challenges of juggling work and relationships.
  • Season 16: Richard's illness is revealed, and the doctors face personal crises while navigating professional challenges, culminating in a global pandemic.
  • Season 17: The COVID-19 pandemic takes over the hospital, and Meredith battles the virus, spending much of the season in a coma and reconnecting with past loved ones in dream sequences.
  • Season 18: Meredith steps into a larger leadership role at Grey Sloan while considering opportunities outside Seattle, and the hospital navigates major staffing shortages.
  • Season 19: A new generation of interns shakes up the hospital, and Meredith moves to Boston to further Alzheimer’s research, stepping away from Seattle.
  • Season 20: Jo and Link’s relationship evolves, and the hospital faces financial struggles that put its future at risk.
  • Season 21: Longtime characters face career and life-altering decisions, while Meredith’s legacy continues to influence the hospital’s culture.
  • Season 22: Grey Sloan Memorial undergoes significant changes in leadership and innovation, and new dynamics emerge among the medical staff as the show continues to evolve.
